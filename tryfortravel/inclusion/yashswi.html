Day Wise Itinerary

Day 1

On Arrival at the airport, proceed to Jatayu National Park, Varkala. After visiting Jatayu National Park, proceed to Kovalam & check into the hotel. This beach town, ranked high among international tourists, is an ideal destination to feel the tropical sun and to feel contented about the golden suntan. Kovalam is a beautiful place to explore its beaches with scenic shores and a beautiful coastline. Water enthusiasts have a lot of beach activities to indulge in like kayaking, swimming, surfing, and skiing. Overnight at Kovalam.

Day 2

After breakfast, we shall proceed to Kanyakumari, the southernmost point of mainland India. It is situated at the confluence of three oceans: the Bay of Bengal, Arabian Sea, and the Indian Ocean. Kanyakumari is the only place on earth where one can see both the sunrise and sunset from the ocean. On the way to Kanyakumari, you can visit Poovar Island. Visit the famous Kanyakumari Temple, Vivekananda Rock (accessible by boat), the statue of Thiruvalluvar on the rock, Gandhi Memorial, wax museum, etc. After sightseeing, proceed to Kovalam and overnight at Kovalam.

Day 3

After breakfast, proceed to Trivandrum for sightseeing. Visit Padmanabha Swami Temple, Museum, Zoo, and Art Gallery. Overnight at Kovalam.
